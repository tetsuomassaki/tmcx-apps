Features

  • Cardiology-specific questionnaire items (chest pain, palpitations, dyspnea, arrhythmia)
  • Systematic 9-section medical interview
  • Automatic A4 PDF generation
  • One-tap upload to WebDAV server
  • Transfer history with retry for failed uploads
  • Highlighted alerts for contrast allergy, anticoagulants, and family history of sudden death
  • Passwords stored securely in iOS Keychain

Perfect For

  • Clinics wanting to digitize paper questionnaires and streamline reception
  • Medical facilities needing cardiology-specific intake forms
  • Facilities sending patient data to in-house servers via WebDAV
