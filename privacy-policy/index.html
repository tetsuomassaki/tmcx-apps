Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

This privacy policy applies to all applications published by TMCX ("we", "us", "our") on the App Store and Google Play.

Data Collection

Our applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or any third-party services. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking — Our apps do not include any analytics SDKs or tracking tools.
  • No advertising — Our apps do not display ads or share data with advertisers.
  • No cloud services — Our apps do not connect to any cloud service operated by us.

Data Stored on Your Device

Some of our apps store settings and user-configured data locally on your device. This data never leaves your device except when explicitly sent to a destination you have configured (e.g., a server you own).

  • Credentials (passwords, tokens) are stored securely in the iOS Keychain.
  • App preferences are stored in local storage (UserDefaults or local database).

Data Transmitted

Some apps may transmit data to servers configured by the user (e.g., WebDAV servers, file servers). This transmission is initiated solely by the user and directed only to the user's specified destination. We have no access to this data.

Device Permissions

Our apps may request access to device features such as Camera or Photo Library. These permissions are used solely for the app's core functionality and can be revoked at any time in your device's Settings.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support page for the relevant app, or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
